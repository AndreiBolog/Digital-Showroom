About Us

Our basic philosophy is to create something that appeals to people in their own special way - which adds something extra to them without adding anything superfluous.

In our design we draw on nomadism - the idea of always walking and being prepared to move on - and to do it with pleasure. Therefore, our designs are designed to fit into different contexts and to be easily moved. They will stand out in any interior, like objects that create both comfort and joy in our everyday lives.

With more than 300 Danish and international design prices, Claus Jensen and Henrik Holbæk, also known as Tools®, are among the most award-winning designers in Europe. Their products are known for their simplicity and ingenuity, often with a little extra or surprising functionality. The designers have created most of the products in the brand Eva Solo.
