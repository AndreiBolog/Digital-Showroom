The Pilgrim Stool

The Pilgrim Stroller is easy to move, no matter where the next destination is. Tabrer's handle is waiting for a firm grip to lift it into the next room - a Pilgrim, a nomad who is always ready to travel. We like to call it 'nomadic design.

White or black food lacquered Handle in polished steel SH46 / 55 x D29cm.

From Designers

“Pilgrim is a series of shovels and a side table that can easily be arranged as needed. And to emphasize their flowability, we have provided them with a hanger. It clearly tells you that there is something that you want to move on, and it's also practical.”

Product Information

Solid lacquered ash tree with a 22 mm seat thickness and 44 mm wide leg. The handle is polished steel and mounted in such a way that the handle does not fall quickly down h as a handle on a bucket generally does. It is also seen by our collecting self-video.
